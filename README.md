# Revenue Growth vs Profit Decline Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project investigates a business problem where a retail company observed **consistent revenue growth while experiencing declining profit margins**. The objective of this analysis was to identify the key drivers behind this trend and uncover actionable insights for decision-makers.

Using **Power BI**, I developed an interactive dashboard to analyze sales performance, discount strategies, product category profitability, and regional performance.

---

## Business Problem
The CEO noticed that:

- Revenue has been increasing steadily over the years
- Profit margins have been declining
- The cause of the decline was unclear

This analysis aims to determine whether **discounting strategies, product categories, or regional performance** are contributing to the decline in profitability.

---

## Key Questions
The analysis focuses on answering the following questions:

- Is increasing discounting impacting profit margins?
- Which product categories drive high revenue but low profitability?
- Which regions generate the most revenue but the lowest profit margins?
- How has revenue, profit margin, and discount strategy evolved over time?

---

## Key KPIs
The dashboard tracks the following metrics:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Profit Margin
- Average Order Value
- Average Discount Value

---

## Dashboard Features
The dashboard includes the following visuals:

### 1. Revenue and Profitability Trends
Shows yearly revenue growth alongside profit margin trends to highlight the divergence between sales growth and profitability.

### 2. Discount vs Profit Margin Trend
Analyzes the relationship between increasing discount rates and declining profit margins.

### 3. Revenue & Profit Margin by Product Category
Identifies categories generating high revenue but weak profit performance.

### 4. Regional Revenue & Profitability
Compares performance across different regions to identify less profitable areas.

### 5. Discount vs Profit Margin by Category
Highlights how discount strategies affect profitability within each category.

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue increased significantly over the years, indicating strong sales performance.
- Profit margins declined steadily despite higher revenue.
- Average discount rates increased year-over-year.
- The **Furniture category generated the highest revenue but the lowest profit margin**, suggesting heavy discounting.
- Higher discount rates strongly correlate with declining profitability.
